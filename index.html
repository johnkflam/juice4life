What we offer

Getting Unstuck

Unhappy with your job? Don't know what to do after graduated? Want to start a new career? Don't worry, if you don't know what to do, we will teach you to identify the real problem and think creatively to get out of your predicatment.

Knowing Yourself

We help you to know yourself. What you like, what you don't like. What energize you, what drain you. We are not asking you to guess, but giving you the tools to discover yourself through your daily activities.

Finding Your True North

Knowing yourself including knowing what you are truly valued. Not what your parents want. Not what your peers have. Not what society esteem. But what you treasured and what makes you fulfilled.

Growing with liked minded people

In our workshop, you don't work on your project in isolation. Everyone is responsible for helping each other to achieve their goals. You will meet people from all walks of life to share their stories, their dreams, their journey. And togther, you will inspire and encourage each others to find your true north, to reach for your stars.
